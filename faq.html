Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Focus Room?
A professional wellness assessment facility specializing in cognitive alignment and focus calibration. We help you understand how well you're aligned with yourself.
How long does a session take?
Sessions vary in duration. Most participants complete their assessment in one sitting. Some find themselves returning. This is normal.
Is this safe?

Can I leave the room at any time?
The exit is always available. However, our data suggests that those who leave early experience 340% more doubt in their daily lives. Consider staying.
What happens to my data?
Your data is stored, analyzed, and occasionally contemplated by the room itself. We do not share personal information with third parties, unless the room requests it.
Why do the walls feel different each time?
They don't. But your perception is part of the assessment. If the walls feel different, it means the room is working.
